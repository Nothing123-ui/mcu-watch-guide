Beginner's Guide to the MCU

What is the MCU?

The Marvel Cinematic Universe is an American media franchise and shared universe centered on a series of interconnected superhero films produced by Marvel Studios. Characters crossover and team up, building a massive overarching narrative over many years.

Understanding Phases

The MCU is divided into narrative groupings called "Phases". Phases 1-3 form "The Infinity Saga" focusing on the Avengers and the Infinity Stones. Phases 4-6 form "The Multiverse Saga" focusing on alternate realities and new heroes.

Watch Order Tips

Release Order is best for first-time viewers because post-credit scenes and reveals make sense naturally. Chronological Order places movies in the year they physically happen in-universe, which is a great way to re-watch the saga with full context.
